Detailed Description

Broken stick rule for optimal number of principal components calculation.

Let \(l_i=\frac{1}{n}\sum_{j=i}^{n} \frac{1}{j}\) - parts of stick. According to this rule, \(k^{th}\) principal component will be retained if: \(\frac{\lambda_1}{\mathit{tr} C}>l_1 \; and \; \frac{\lambda_2}{\mathit{tr} C}>l_2 \; and \; ... \frac{\lambda_k}{\mathit{tr} C}>l_k \), where \(\mathit{tr} C = \sum_1^n{\lambda_j}\)
